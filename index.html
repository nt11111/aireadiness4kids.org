What We Do

Six programs, one mission

From free downloadable lessons to in-school workshops and parent nights — meeting kids, educators, and families wherever they are.

Program 01

Free Curriculum Library

A freely downloadable K-12 curriculum in three age-appropriate tracks. No login. No paywall. Lesson plans, slide decks, facilitator guides, and worksheets.

Program 02

In-School Workshops

Facilitated sessions delivered in schools — from a 60-minute Express Workshop to a 6-hour Full-Day Immersive with a capstone project.

Program 03

Parent Information Nights

90-minute evening events helping parents learn what AI is, how kids use it, the risks, and how to have productive conversations at home — in English & Spanish.

How It Works

From download to classroom in four steps

1

Choose a track

Pick AI Explorers, Investigators, or Architects based on your students' grade level.

2

Download free

Grab lesson plans, slide decks, facilitator guides, and worksheets — no account needed.

3

Teach or book us

Run it yourself, or invite a certified facilitator to deliver a workshop in your school.

4

Build the habit

Students leave thinking critically — questioning, checking, and using AI responsibly.

Our Vision

By 2030, responsible AI literacy in every classroom

  • 50,000+ students reached annually

    Our Year-5 North Star — peer-to-peer delivery that multiplies our reach.

  • Curriculum in English & Spanish

    Equity means reaching every family, in the language they speak at home.

  • 100+ certified student ambassadors

    High schoolers leading responsible-AI workshops at their own schools.
