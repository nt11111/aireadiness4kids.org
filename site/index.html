Why families and teachers trust ARK

You should know exactly what your kids are learning and how. Here is what makes our approach safe, clear, and ready for the classroom or the kitchen table.

Reviewed by real teachers

Every module is tested and reviewed by active classroom teachers before it is published, so lessons are practical, accurate, and age appropriate.

Safety and privacy first

No student logins. No data collected from children. Lessons teach kids to protect their privacy and treat others kindly, online and off.

Conversations for home

Parent Information Nights, offered in English and Spanish, give you the words and confidence to talk about AI with your child at home.

Ready to teach

Lesson plans, slide decks, facilitator guides, and worksheets come finished and easy to run, even if you have no technical background.

Grade by grade

Three tracks meet students where they are, from gentle discovery in early grades to real ethics and agency in high school.

Free, and staying that way

Curriculum and core programs are free to students and schools. Grants and donations keep it open to everyone, with no paywall.

What we do

Six programs, one mission

From free downloadable lessons to in-school workshops and parent nights, we meet kids, educators, and families wherever they are.

Program 01

Free Curriculum Library

A downloadable K-12 curriculum in three grade-banded tracks. No login. No paywall. Lesson plans, slide decks, facilitator guides, and worksheets.

Program 02

In-School Workshops

Facilitated sessions delivered in schools, from a 60 minute Express Workshop to a 6 hour Full-Day Immersive with a capstone project.

Program 03

Parent Information Nights

90 minute evening events that help parents understand what AI is, how kids use it, the risks, and how to talk about it at home. English and Spanish.
