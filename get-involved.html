Student Ambassador Program

High schoolers leading the way

Students in grades 9–12 serve as peer advocates for responsible AI at their own schools. Peer-to-peer delivery is more credible and relatable for K-12 audiences — one trained teen can run workshops for hundreds of classmates.

  • One-day certification training

    Get equipped with everything you need to facilitate confidently.

  • One workshop per semester

    Facilitate at your own school or in your community.

  • Recognition & co-authorship

    Receive a letter of recognition and co-authorship credit on content you contribute.

Apply to be an ambassador

Why Student Ambassadors?

Ambassadors create an organic reach multiplier — one trained teen can run workshops for hundreds of classmates without needing a staff facilitator present. This mirrors the model used successfully by Cyber for Youth.
